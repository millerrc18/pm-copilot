# Procurement Dashboard

Use Procurement to review material spend and identify supplier and commodity concentration. Filters align with IFS Materials exports.

## Key metrics

- Material spend in the selected period.
- Top supplier and top commodity by spend.
- Unique part count.

## Tips

- Use the supplier filter to focus on a single vendor.
- Use part number search to isolate a part or assembly.
- Adjust date range to match receipt or purchasing windows.
