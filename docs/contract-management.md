# Contract management

Contracts represent the commercial scope of a program. Each contract contains delivery periods, milestones, and unit shipments.

## Add a contract

1. Open the related program.
2. Select **Add contract**.
3. Enter the contract code, start/end dates, fiscal year, and planned quantity.
4. Save to unlock cost, milestone, and delivery unit imports.

## Edit contract information

1. Open the contract detail page.
2. Select **Edit contract**.
3. Update contract dates, quantities, or pricing.
4. Save changes.

## Delete a contract

1. Open the contract detail page.
2. Select **Delete contract**.
3. Confirm the removal.

> Deleting a contract removes associated milestones and delivery units. Export data first if needed.

## Track performance

Use the contract summary to monitor revenue, cost, and margin. Visit [Dashboard metrics](/docs/dashboard-metrics) for KPI definitions.
