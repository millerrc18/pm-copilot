# Dashboard metrics

The program dashboard consolidates cost, revenue, and delivery KPIs. Use this guide to interpret each metric and follow up quickly.

## Revenue metrics

- **Total revenue**: Sum of revenue across all contract periods.
- **Revenue to date**: Revenue recognized through the current period.
- **Avg revenue per unit**: Revenue divided by units delivered.

## Cost metrics

- **Total cost**: Labor + material + overhead costs across periods.
- **Cost to date**: Costs posted through the current period.
- **Avg cost per unit**: Cost divided by units delivered.

## Delivery metrics

- **Units delivered**: Count of shipped delivery units.
- **On-time milestones**: Percentage of milestones completed before the due date.
- **Delivery health**: Combined indicator of schedule risk and backlog.

## Alerts & signals

- **Risk flags** appear when costs spike or delivery units slip.
- **Variance alerts** highlight gaps between planned vs actual quantities.
