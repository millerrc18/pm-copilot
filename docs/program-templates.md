# Program templates

Program templates will let teams launch new initiatives with pre-built milestones, contract structures, and reporting packs.

## What to expect

- Curated templates for common contract types and delivery models.
- Predefined milestone schedules and import templates.
- Recommended KPIs for cost, delivery, and margin tracking.

**Status:** Coming soon. We are validating template types with early customers.
